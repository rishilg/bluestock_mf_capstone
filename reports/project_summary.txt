Mutual Fund Analytics Capstone Project

Project Objective:
To analyze mutual fund performance, risk characteristics,
portfolio allocation, and sector exposure using Python,
SQL, and Power BI.

Datasets Used:
1. Fund Master
2. NAV History
3. AUM by Fund House
4. Monthly SIP Inflows
5. Category Inflows
6. Industry Folio Count
7. Scheme Performance
8. Investor Transactions
9. Portfolio Holdings
10. Benchmark Indices

Key Metrics Computed:
- CAGR
- Volatility
- Sharpe Ratio
- Maximum Drawdown
- Value at Risk (VaR 95%)

Portfolio Optimization:
Markowitz Mean-Variance Optimization

Dashboard Pages:
1. Executive Overview
2. Fund Performance Analysis
3. Performance Filters & Deep Dive
4. Risk Analytics Insights
5. Portfolio Optimization
6. Sector Analysis

Outcome:
Successfully developed an end-to-end mutual fund analytics
dashboard providing actionable investment insights.
